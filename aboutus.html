ABOUT US

Bluejack Awquarium is a new tourist destination that house around 500 kinds of sea creature. We also provide a sea-themed show and exotic seafood in our restaurant.

Welcome to Bluejack AWquarium, a family recreation center and the first indoor wildlife conservation in Indonesia endorsed by Taman Safari Indonesia in collaboration with Aquaria KLCC Malaysia. Bluejack AWquarium present a new concept of outdoor vacation that displays the richness of Indonesia's underwater nature that is colorful, beautiful and mysterious that will make you feel like you're on vacation.


Bluejack AWquarium also present world class theatre show "Pearl of The North Sea" produced by Peter Wilson, famous Australian producer. Pearl of The North Sea tells about a queen which is cursed to become The Sea Queen combine with Mermaid Dance, music and technological sophistication. At the end of the show, you can capture the moment with the Princess and Mermaids behind the aquarium.


After you explore the aquarium, you can enjoy fresh seafood while watching penguins. You also can interact and give them food at the Restaurant.
