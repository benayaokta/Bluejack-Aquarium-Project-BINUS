Grand Opening


Celebrating its 30th birthday, Taman Safari Indonesia Group is proud to present Bluejack AWquarium, a leading project of a family conservation and recreation institution in Indonesia. Bluejack AWquarium is located in West Jakarta which will be opened to the public in July 2019.

Aqua Camp


Where children and families can spend their night underwater to experience sleeping with aquatic and nocturnal animals. In the Aqua Camp, participants are invited to interact with animals closely, learn to care for them, be taught by international facilitators, and have the opportunity to conduct question and answer sessions with animal experts. Education is provided in a fun and interactive way to maximize educational activities.

Pearl of the North Sea


Tells a story about the love of the King to his only daughter. This story adapted from a folklore in a Kingdom at South Java. Prepare to be fascinated with amazing performance, combination of stage illusions, tricks and underwaterdances.

Touch Pool


Show your love by caressing these animals! Interact with Bamboo Sharks, blue sea starts, snails and much more.
